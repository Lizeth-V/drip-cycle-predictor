Why is manually tracking your period important?

By eliminating the use of period tracking apps, we can create a safer environment for people to track their cycle.
Due to the recent privacy violations of established well-known period tracking apps, logging your personal information has become more
concerning than before. We want to allow people to have control and track their own periods.
